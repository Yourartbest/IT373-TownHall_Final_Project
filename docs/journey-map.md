# Customer Journey Map — Newark AI Town Hall

This journey map reflects the experience of our primary persona: **Angela, a Newark parent with low tech literacy.** It also captures key experiential touchpoints relevant to educators and student volunteers.

---

# Stage 1 — Awareness
**Triggers:**  
- Sees a flyer at school or library  
- Hears about AI workshops from a friend  
- Finds a post on Facebook  

**Actions:**  
- Visits the Town Hall website from her phone  

**Thoughts:**  
“This sounds useful, but I don't know much about AI.”  

**Feelings:**  
Curious but uncertain  

**Opportunities:**  
- Simple homepage explaining “What is this?”  
- Clear CTA: **Browse Upcoming Events**  
- Plain-language descriptions  

---

# Stage 2 — Exploration
**Actions:**  
- Scrolls through events  
- Reads simple descriptions  
- Looks for labels like “Beginner-Friendly” or “Family-Friendly”  

**Thoughts:**  
“Is this event meant for someone like me?”  

**Feelings:**  
Overwhelmed if too much text  

**Pain Points:**  
- Hard-to-understand event titles  
- No sense of difficulty level  

**Opportunities:**  
- Filters (Beginner, Educator, Youth, Free)  
- “What You’ll Learn” bullet lists  
- Short video explainers  

---

# Stage 3 — Registration
**Actions:**  
- Taps an event  
- Fills out a simple form  
- Chooses to receive SMS reminders  

**Thoughts:**  
“I hope this is easy and quick.”  

**Feelings:**  
Relieved if the form is short  

**Pain Points:**  
- Too many required fields  
- No confirmation message  

**Opportunities:**  
- One-minute registration  
- Auto-confirmation screen  
- SMS reminder automation  

---

# Stage 4 — Attendance
**Actions:**  
- Arrives at event or joins online  
- Shows confirmation message at check-in  

**Thoughts:**  
“I hope I can follow along.”  

**Feelings:**  
Anxious at first, hopeful during session  

**Opportunities:**  
- Friendly welcome  
- Clear agenda  
- Hands-on mini-activities  

---

# Stage 5 — Post-Event Engagement
**Actions:**  
- Receives follow-up email/SMS  
- Reads a recap  
- Joins Discord community  
- Registers for another event  

**Thoughts:**  
“I want to stay involved.”  

**Feelings:**  
Empowered and supported  

**Opportunities:**  
- Thank-you email with resources  
- “Next recommended events”  
- Auto-post event recap in Discord
